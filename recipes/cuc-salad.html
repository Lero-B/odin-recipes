Cucumber Salad

Quick and easy Cucumber salad that taste fantastic

A homemade recipe that's so easy and quick to make


This WAS an easy and quick recipe for your basic lasagna, HOWEVER, I did alter it a bit! :) I added chopped onion and 3 cloves minced fresh garlic to the ground beef while it was browning, along with a teaspoon or so of Paula Deen's House Seasoning (Jane's Krazy Mixed Up Salt and pepper would work, too).

Ingredients

  • ½ cup reduced-fat sour cream (such as Daisy®)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 3 cucumbers, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise
  • ½ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix sour cream, lemon juice, and dill together in a bowl. Add cucumbers and onion; stir to combine. Season with salt and black pepper.

Nutritional information

Per Serving:50 calories; protein 1.5g; carbohydrates 6.3g; fat 2.6g; cholesterol 7.9mg; sodium 11.6mg.
